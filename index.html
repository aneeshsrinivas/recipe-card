Ultimate Chocolate Cake

Yield: 8 servings (2-layer 9-inch cake) • 12-16 slices

Tip: Ensure ingredients (eggs, buttermilk, butter) are at room temperature

Contains: Gluten, Dairy, Eggs
Prep Time: 30 mins
Cook Time: 40 mins
Servings:

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups (220g) all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup (65g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1½ cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk
  • ½ cup (115g) melted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Sift together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, buttermilk, melted butter, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Stir in hot water until combined (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour evenly into pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes until toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  7. Frost with chocolate buttercream or your favorite frosting.

Chef's Notes

Storage

Keeps for 3 days at room temp, 5 days refrigerated, or freeze up to 2 months.

Substitutions

  • Buttermilk: 1 cup milk + 1 tbsp vinegar
  • Eggs: ½ cup applesauce per egg
  • Gluten-free: Use 1:1 GF flour blend

Variations

  • Add 1 tsp instant coffee to intensify chocolate
  • Mix in 1 cup chocolate chips
  • Top with fresh berries
